Problem & Solution

The Problem

Fragmented Pet Industry: Pet owners face disconnected loyalty programs, limited payment options, and lack of unified rewards across different retailers.

Memecoin Fatigue: Most memecoins have no utility beyond speculation, leading to short-lived projects and disappointed communities.

Entertainment Gap: Pet-focused content lacks interactive, blockchain-integrated storytelling that engages modern audiences.

The Larry Solution

$LARRY creates a unified ecosystem that solves these problems through:

  • Payment Integration: Seamless crypto payments at partner pet stores worldwide
  • Unified Loyalty: Single loyalty token ($LPOOP) usable across all partner retailers
  • Engaging Content: Original storytelling through mininovelas, NFTs, and interactive media
  • Real Utility: Actual use cases beyond trading - shop, earn, engage

How to Store $LARRY Safely

Best Practices

  • Hardware Wallets: For large amounts, consider Ledger or Trezor
  • Seed Phrase Security: Write down your 12/24 word seed phrase on paper, never digitally
  • Multiple Copies: Store backup seed phrases in separate secure locations
  • Never Share: Never give your seed phrase or private keys to anyone
  • Verify Addresses: Always double-check wallet addresses before sending

Common Scams to Avoid

⚠️ Watch Out For:
  • Fake support accounts asking for seed phrases
  • Phishing websites with similar URLs
  • Unsolicited DMs offering "help" or "airdrops"
  • Fake contract addresses

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is $LARRY?
$LARRY is a Solana-based cryptocurrency token that powers an ecosystem focused on the pet industry. It combines memecoin culture with real-world utility through payment integration, loyalty rewards, and original media content.
Is this just another memecoin?
While $LARRY started with memecoin culture and storytelling, it's building toward real utility. The roadmap includes payment gateway integration with pet stores, a loyalty token system, and media production. This is a long-term project with sustainable revenue models.
Why Solana?
Solana offers fast transactions, low fees, and a growing ecosystem. For real-world payments at pet stores, transaction speed and cost are critical. Solana's infrastructure makes micro-transactions viable.
What makes Larry different from other dog coins?
Larry has a three-pillar approach: FinTech (payments), Media (content), and Retail (pet stores). Most dog coins focus only on trading. Larry is building an actual ecosystem with revenue streams beyond token appreciation.

Technical

What is the contract address?
The official contract address will be announced at launch and prominently displayed on larrythedog.com, this documentation site, and all official social media channels. Always verify from multiple official sources.
Are there any buy/sell taxes?
No. $LARRY has 0% transaction taxes. There are no fees for buying or selling beyond standard blockchain gas fees.
Is the contract audited?
A professional audit is scheduled for Q1 2026. The contract code is open source for community review. We encourage technical community members to examine the code and report any concerns.
Is liquidity locked?
Yes. Liquidity is permanently locked at launch by burning LP tokens. This means liquidity cannot be removed by anyone, including the team.

What is the total supply?
1,000,000,000 (1 billion) $LARRY tokens. 50% (500 million) are immediately burned at launch, making the circulating supply effectively 500 million.

No. There is 0% team allocation. The team does not hold any $LARRY tokens from the initial supply. Community (15%) and Marketing (10%) allocations are vested over 12 and 6 months respectively, preventing sudden dumps.
What is $LPOOP?
